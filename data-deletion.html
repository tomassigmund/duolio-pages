User Data Deletion

This page is intended for Meta / Facebook Login data deletion instructions.

If you used Facebook Login with Duolio and want your associated data deleted, follow these steps:

  1. Open Facebook and go to Settings & privacy.
  2. Open Settings.
  3. Open Apps and Websites.
  4. Find Duolio in the list of connected apps.
  5. Choose Remove to revoke access.
  6. If you want us to delete any remaining account data stored by Duolio, send a request to support@duolio.app with the subject Facebook Data Deletion Request.

What to include in the request

  1. Your contact e-mail.
  2. The sign-in method you used, for example Facebook Login.
  3. Any details that help us identify your account safely.

Processing

After we verify the request, we will delete or anonymize the relevant account data unless we must keep part of it for legal, security, or fraud prevention reasons.

Replace support@duolio.app with your real support e-mail before publishing this page publicly.
