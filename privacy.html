Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 23, 2026

Duolio helps people choose movies, date ideas, and travel plans together. This Privacy Policy explains what data we process when you create an account, sign in with third-party providers, or use the app.

Information we collect

  • Account information such as e-mail address and authentication provider.
  • Basic profile information you add in the app.
  • Relationship, pair, or group data needed to make shared decisions work.
  • Session activity such as swipes, shortlist items, and recommendation history.
  • Technical data needed for notifications, sign-in, security, and diagnostics.

How we use your data

  • To create and maintain your account.
  • To sync your choices with your partner or group.
  • To deliver features such as social sign-in and push notifications.
  • To improve recommendations, reliability, and product quality.

Third-party services

Duolio may use third-party services such as Supabase, Google, Apple, Facebook, Firebase Cloud Messaging, TMDB, Google Places, and other infrastructure or content providers needed to operate the app.

Data retention

We keep account and app usage data only for as long as necessary to operate the service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

Your choices

You can request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data. For Facebook-related deletion instructions, see the User Data Deletion page.

Contact

For privacy questions or data requests, contact: support@duolio.app
